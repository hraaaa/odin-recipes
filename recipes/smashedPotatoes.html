Easy AirFryer Smashed Potatoes (With dipping sauce)

- by Hreetik Arora



Description

This recipe is a slightly modified version of this recipe, which involves shallow frying.
I wanted to give this recipe a healthier touch, but still be equally as tasty. Hope you enjoy it!

Ingredients:

  • Small Potatoes
  • Seasoning(Salt and Pepper)
  • Garlic(or Garlic Powder)
  • Paprika
  • Clarified Butter(Ghee)
  • Curd
  • Lemon
  • Coriander Leaves

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a large pot. Add Salt and a bit of Pepper to it.
  2. Wash your potatoes and add them to this boiling water(no need to peel the skin off)
  3. Boil the potatoes on medium-high heat for around 7mins, and check if they're cooked.
    They should be soft enough for a knife to pass but should hold their shape.
  4. Strain the boiled potatoes and let them cool down.
  5. Take a knife and make 2 slashes in a plus form on the potatoes, and then use a Glass/Bowl's bottom to flatten them to about 1cm thickness.
  6. Mince your garlic and spread it on the top side of the smashed potatoes. Add Paprika, Salt and Pepper according to your preference.
  7. Pre-heat your AirFryer at its max temp for around 5 mins.
  8. Spread a bit of clarified butter on the potatoes using a brush and put them in the air fryer.
  9. You only need to crispen up the top layer of the potatoes and cook the garlic. Hence put the AirFryer on its max temperature for 6 minutes>
    Check the potatoes 4 minutes in and adjust accordingly.
  10. Take them out and let them rest for 5 mins on a wire rack, so that the underside doesn't get soggy.

Dipping Sauce:

Take your curd in a bowl, and add minced garlic, lemon juice, lightly chopped coriander leaves and season to taste.
Mix everything in the bowl using a spoon/fork, and Voila! Your dipping sauce is ready!

Enjoy your healthy Smashed Potatoes with Dipping Sauce! Serve them as a Side Dish or a an appetizer.
